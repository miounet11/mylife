# Visual style bible — page illustrations

## Palette

- Paper: `#f7f7f5` / white
- Ink: `#1d2129` / `#606770`
- Brand blue: `#3b5998` / soft `#e7f3ff`
- Hairline: `#e4e6eb`
- Avoid pure black fills, neon, gold-on-purple “mystical” kitsch

## Composition

- 16:9 educational diagrams for page heroes
- Clear hierarchy: title area → diagram → caption zone
- Chinese labels short (2–8 chars per node)
- Soft shadows, rounded cards, Linear product feel

## Forbidden

- Crystal balls, tarot, ghosts, blood-red “灾”
- Absolute fate slogans (必发、铁口、改命)
- Low-res stock photos of random “masters”
- Cluttered 20-node graphs

## Allowed metaphors

- Paths, windows, K-line rhythm, pillars, five-element cycles (educational)
- Checklists, 今天/7天/30天 rails
- Report chapters as map layers
